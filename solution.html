Solution

From a distance, the Live Life Company uses camera data to monitor heart
rate, respiration, and blood oxygenation in homes. The idea will be used in real-life
settings such as your home all across the world. The Vital Cam detects minute
changes in video frames, such as when a person chest rises and falls as they breathe,
as they walk around the house and many more. It also monitors minor variations in
their skin's pinkness, which it uses to calculate their pulse. However, the cameras
would be able to monitor everyone in their immediate vicinity. It does not only detect
vital sign but also acts like a normal cctv where it records movements and can still
keep you home safe.
