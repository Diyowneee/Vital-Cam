Camera

Cameras allow you to watch events unfold in real time, and recorders save material
for later viewing. The skin tone changes when the heart beats because of the increased
blood flowing through the veins. These minute skin color changes are invisible to the
naked eye, but Vital Cam camera algorithms amplify them and use the frequency of the
color changes to calculate an accurate pulse rate signal. It uses an IP (Internet protocol)
camera that performs the same functions as analog cameras but with far more capabilities.
IP cameras provide clearer, higher-resolution images as well as more versatile features
such as remote zooming and repositioning. They also allow you to view footage through
a web browser. This allows you to receive notifications when your cameras detect
something unusual, such as activity inside your home at 3 a.m. with detection of vital
signs, and see it live from anywhere using a computer or smartphone. The camera
focuses on the rise and fall of the chest and stomach in order to compute an accurate
breathing rate. Any skin type can benefit from the technology. During motion, it uses facial
tracking to acquire an accurate assessment. This guarantees that a pulse and breathing
rate can be determined at all times.
